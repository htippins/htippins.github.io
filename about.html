A little bit about me...

Hello, my name is Holly. I currently work as a mental health nurse in the NHS. I started to re-evaluate my future as a nurse towards the end of 2021. I'd worked throughout the covid-19 pandemic and experienced first-hand the crisis the mental health system in the UK is facing (and more broadly the NHS). I made the decision to pursue a career in web development after consideration about what the future of nursing looked like to me.

My life-long interest in computers and tech drove me towards wanting to dig deeper into the world of programming. I started my journey using self-teaching using platforms like Udemy and freeCodeCamp, and I haven't looked back.

I started formal education in January 2022 at the Univeristy of Northumbria to study Computer Science with a focus on Data Analytics. I cut down my hours at work and focus the other 50% of my time on developing my programming skills.

I have taken a brief break in my studies to embark on the CODE Lanacshire bootcamp with the intention to start working full-time as a developer upon completion.
